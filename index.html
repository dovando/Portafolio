What we do

We source high-quality coffee directly from local farms in Guatemala and the Dominican Republic, carefully package it, and distribute it both locally and internationally.

Our focus is on building strong relationships with growers and delivering fresh, great-tasting coffee to customers around the world.

Why Guatemala and Dominican Republic Coffee

Both countries produce beans grown in ideal conditions of altitude, climate, and rich soil.

Guatemalan coffee is famous for its bold flavors and chocolate notes, while Dominican coffee is known for its smooth and aromatic profile.

According to International Coffee Reports, high-altitude beans can improve flavor complexity by up to 20%.
