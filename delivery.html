Home Delivery

We offer fast and reliable home delivery so you can enjoy fresh coffee without leaving your home. Our delivery service is available within Vancouver, BC and nearby areas with flexible time slots to fit your schedule.

Fresh Coffee in Just Three Steps:

  1. Order: Choose your favorite coffee products, preferred blends, roasts, and package sizes, and add them to your cart.
  2. Confirm: Review your chosen coffees, confirm your delivery address, and complete your payment to prepare your order for shipment.
  3. Deliver: We carefully pack your coffee to keep it fresh and flavorful, then deliver it straight to your door so you can enjoy every cup at its best.

Export options

For international orders, we prepare all required export documentation, including commercial invoices and certificates of origin, to ensure compliance with international trade regulations.

We work with reliable logistics partners to offer air and ground shipping options based on destination, cost, and delivery time.

All export orders are carefully packed using food-safe materials designed to protect the coffee and preserve freshness during long-distance transport, so your shipment arrives in excellent condition.

Policies

  • Delivery: Orders are processed from Monday to Friday and are usually delivered within 24 to 48 hours for local delivery. International shipments may take longer depending on customs processing and transportation times.
  • Returns: If there is any issue with your order, such as damaged packaging or incorrect items, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery. We will review the situation and offer a replacement or refund according to our quality and safety standards.
